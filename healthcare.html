Connecting people. Powering business.

ANFC, we specialize in delivering comprehensive talent and workforce solutions to medical and dental organizations. Our services are designed to help organizations attract and retain exceptional professionals, while maintaining compliance, efficiency, and excellence in patient care.

Recruiting

We understand that every healthcare role impacts lives. This is why we take a people-first approach to recruitment. Our team connects healthcare organizations with highly qualified professionals who not only meet skill requirements, but also align with your mission and values.

We provide…

  • Targeted recruitment strategies for niche and high-demand roles
  • Specialized searches that crack the code on hard to fill positions
  • Permanent, contract, and interim placement solutions
  • Medical physicians, dentists, and support staff (medical/dental assistants, receptionists, billing and customer service professionals…and more)

Strategic Sourcing

Finding the right talent requires more than job postings. It requires proactive engagement and strategic insight. In addition to other tools, our sourcing experts leverage deep industry networks to identify top healthcare professionals, sometime before they enter the job market.

We provide…

  • Targeted candidate sourcing
  • Pipeline development for critical and high-volume roles
  • Market intelligence and compensation insights

Talent Management

Strong HR practices are the foundation of a healthy workplace. Our consulting team partners with healthcare organizations to build effective, compliant, and people-focused HR strategies. Whether you need support with a project or an ongoing advisory relationship, we help organizations strengthen their workforce and streamline operations.

We provide…

  • Workforce planning, organizational design and succession management
  • HR audit and compliance support
  • Employee handbook and policy development
  • Performance management and employee engagement
  • Complete background checks
  • Training, leadership development, and retention strategies
  • Onboarding and retention strategies
  • Employee engagement programs
  • Compensation and benefits benchmarking
